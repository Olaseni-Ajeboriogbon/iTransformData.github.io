About Me

I am a results-driven WordPress web developer and data analyst with a strong passion for building fast, visually engaging digital experiences and transforming data into meaningful insights. My journey into tech started from a deep curiosity for creativity and problem-solving, which has evolved into a career focused on delivering practical, high-impact solutions.

I specialise in developing responsive and performance-optimised websites, while also leveraging tools like Power BI and data analysis techniques to uncover trends, build forecasts, and support data-driven decision-making. I enjoy working at the intersection of design, technology, and analytics, where I can both create and interpret.
In addition to my core skills, I have a growing interest in AI and automation, particularly in building intelligent workflows that streamline processes and enhance productivity. I am constantly exploring ways to integrate data, automation, and user experience into cohesive systems that deliver real value.

I am committed to continuous learning and always looking for opportunities to apply my skills to solve real-world problems, contribute to innovative projects, and grow within a forward-thinking organization
